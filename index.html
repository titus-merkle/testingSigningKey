Scoring

A win in each knockout round is worth: Round of 16 = 1pt · Quarter-final = 2pts · Semi-final = 4pts · Third-place play-off = 3pts · Final = 8pts.
Each player holds two teams. A team is struck through once it loses (knocked out). A player's name is struck through once both their teams are out.
Winners are read from penalties → extra time → full time. Data auto-updates from the open-source openfootball World Cup dataset — no login, no key.
